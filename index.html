About

My name is Paolo. I was born in 1994 in Trieste (TS), a little beautiful city in the North-East of Italy, where I attended my Primary, Middle and High school. I then moved to Torino (TO) to become an Engineer at the Politecnico di Torino, where I got my Bachelor (Telecommunications Engineering) and Master Degree (Mechatronic Engineering). I'm currently in love with drones and robotics. In 2017, while studying, I started developing a project on autonomous UAV for a local company. In my free time I study electric guitar, I write songs and I practise a lot of gymnastic. I succesfully completed the Master ICT and Advanced Projecting (2 years) to improve my skills. I am currently working with Dr. Kostas Alexis at the Autonomous Robots Lab ARL

Projects

ARS (Autonomous Remote Sensing)

The main goal of the ARS (Autonomous Remote Sensing) project was to design adn prototype a drone capable of carrying out autonomous inspection of confined spaces, in particular of tunnels. Exploiting the concept of Data Muling, the UAV is able to navigate inside a GPS-denied environment without any help from the operator, collecting data from pre-disposed BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) modules and, in post-processing, create a 3D model of the inspected environment with an error of ~1mm.

Link to the video summarizing the project here.

Autonomous Indoor UAV Navigation & Mapping

A project for indoor, unknown, GPS-denied environment navigation of UAVs. The main used sensors are a 2D Lidar laser scanner range finder and a Pixhawk flight controller. All computation are performed onboard.

Link to the video here.

Arduino mini CNC plotter with recycled CD/DVD Drives

A simple mini CNC plotter realized with some old CD Drives found in the basement. I initially used this guide as a reference, then I implemented my own configuration. You can see my tiny printer in action here.
